What is Rekord.AI?

A web-based and access-controlled information system that provides a centralized repository of any information in different formats. It aims to collect data, consolidate and transform into informative reports that any top management would love to see. Rekord.AI is your modern File System designed to be responsive and user-friendly in managing your information assets. It has a Form Builder feature to build electronic forms as a tool to collect and process data or sign digitally and automate business processes with its built-in data analytics.
